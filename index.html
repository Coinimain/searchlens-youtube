HOW IT WORKS

Search normally, then refine.

SearchLens adds a subtle launcher to YouTube search-result pages. Open it when you need more control, apply your filters, and keep browsing.

  1. 1
    Search

    Start from the extension popup or search YouTube normally.

  2. 2
    Refine

    Open SearchLens and choose the filters that matter.

  3. 3
    Explore

    Scroll for more results and SearchLens reapplies your local filters.
